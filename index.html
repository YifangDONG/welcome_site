    Dear international students,

    You are about to become new students from TEM or TSP. You probably have a lot of questions to ask.This website was created to answer these questions and give you a preview of the life on the campus.

    Welcom is a club composed by students from both schools. Our goal is to welcome you on the campus, help you during your stay and, of course, introduce you to the social life of TMSP. We will organize a certain number of events, such as parties, visits on Paris during week ends, etc.

    You can contact us either by e-mail (welcom@tem-tsp.eu) or by Facebook on the Welcom’ page or on the Welcom’ internationnal group in which you will find a place plenty of welcomers and other foreign students eager to help you.

    Each year, lots of international students come on our campus and leave with a wonderful experience. You may be the next one! The whole Welcom team is waiting for your arrival on the campus.

    Laura DENNER, President of Welcom’ for the year 2015-2016

    PS: If you already know your flight schedule, please start being ready for your arrival and register in the link of the article below, in order to prepare your room, keys and the pick-up service if it is possible. This is really important for us !!

    Infos about your arrival – Link to register for your arrival

    Posted on June 13, 2015 by celine Posted in Uncategorized

    You are about to become TMSP students in September. Here are some pieces of information for your arrival:

    REGISTRATION

    When you have your flight schedule, please register here. Please send us an email (welcom@tem-tsp.eu) if the link doesn’t work! If you don’t register, we won’t be able to prepare your room keys and your arrival. Please register for one person and not for a group. We remind you that we need EVERY piece of information asked in the form. If you have already registered and want to change something, please send a mail to welcom@tem-tsp.eu.

    PICK UP SERVICE

    A special Welcom’ bus can pick you up on September, 1st at Roissy Charles de Gaulle, either at noon or at the end of the day. It will cost you 30€/person. For people who can not arrive on those two days, you can still contact us by sending an e-mail to welcom@tem-tsp.eu, and we will see what we can do to help you, or eventually to pick you up if and only if a welcom’ member is available. Be careful, the registration doesn’t mean that you will be picked up. You have to wait for a confirmation by mail. Moreover, please try to take a plane ticket with an arrival during the office hours (9am to 5pm). For any problem or question about the pick-up service, please contact Welcom’ members at this e-mail address : welcom@tem-tsp.eu

    PS : If you arrive with or without a pickup, you have to arrive during the office hours. If you have a late flight, you have to take a night at the hotel before coming to the campus.

    YOUR ARRIVAL

    Here is the list of things you have to do at your arrival with Welcom’ members:

    - Take your keys and go to your room
    - Fill in a contract for bed linen kit (15€ for the Maisel, optional)
    - Fill in a contract for dishes kit
    - Take your computer to the MINET association who provides you with an Internet connexion (6pm to 7.30pm)
    - Campus visit

    If you have any other questions, please join us on our Welcom’ page or Welcom’ International Group.

    The association which is in charge of international students from Télécom Business School and Télécom SudParis.
